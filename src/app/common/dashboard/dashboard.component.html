Alternative Wood Blinds Offer Beauty That Stands Up to Humidity

The classic look of plantation blinds looks great in any home. But most wood blinds can be easily damaged when placed in rooms with increased moisture and humidity. That’s where Hunter Douglas EverWood® Blinds from Louver Shop come in.

These horizontal blinds are a smart alternative to hardwood blinds, blending traditional style with the convenience of modern materials that resist warping or damage from humid conditions.

Available in either realistic TruGrain® wood finishes or Renditions™ solid colors, EverWood® Blinds are an attractive, durable addition to any room for years to come.

Your Louver Shop consultant can help you find the right EverWood® style to complement your home. Whether your style is traditional, contemporary, or somewhere in between, we can help.

The Look of Wood Blinds, the Durability of Modern Materials

EverWood® blinds are made from a proprietary, PVC-free polymer that is easy to clean. This material also resists damage from extreme heat, humidity, moisture, and direct sunlight. That makes them perfect for kitchens, bathrooms, and laundry rooms, spaces that might otherwise present problems for traditional wood blinds.

EverWood’s de-Light™ system improves privacy and light control by hiding cord holes. That means these blinds can block more than 75 percent of the sun’s damaging UV rays when closed. And reduces the chance of the sun fading your home’s furniture, artwork, or flooring. You might even see savings on your energy bill.
